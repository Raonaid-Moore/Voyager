Activities

Zero Gravity

Get ready to defy gravity with our Zero Gravity experience at Voyager Hotel! This one-of-a-kind experience offers guests the chance to float, flip and soar in the unique environment of microgravity, creating unforgettable memories and endless fun.

 Moon Walk

Our Moon Walk activity offers guests a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to walk on the surface of the Moon, just like the astronauts of the Apollo missions. This exhilarating experience is designed to be both fun and educational, providing an unforgettable addition to your stay.

 Celestial Spa

Welcome to our Venusian Celestial Spa, the ultimate relaxation destination located within the stunning Voyager Venus. Nestled amidst the surreal landscape of Venus, our spa offers a unique blend of advanced space-age therapies and timeless relaxation techniques, ensuring a rejuvenating experience like no other.
Europa Splashdown

Dive into a world of excitement and wonder at Europa Splashdown, the most extraordinary water park across the cosmos, located at the Voyager Jupiter. Harnessing the icy and watery wonders of Europa, our water park offers a unique blend of thrilling rides, relaxing pools and intergalactic fun for guests of all ages.

 Martian Rover Rally

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure with Martian Rover Rally, an exhilarating rover racing experience at the Voyager Mars. Explore the rugged red terrain of the Martian landscape as you compete in high speed races across the planet's most challenging and captivating courses.

 Red Planet Cave Expedition

Dive into the depths of Mars with the Red Planet Cave Expedition, an awe-inspiring cave-diving adventure offered exclusively at the Voyager Hotel. This activity promises an unforgettable journey into the mysterious and uncharted realm beneath the Martian surface.
