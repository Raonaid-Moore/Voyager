Accommodation

This luxurious bedroom will fit all of your needs during your stay with these soundproof walls and comfort, with top of the line mattresses. There are 800 of these rooms available throughout the hotel with 5 presidential suites
The fine cuisine you will find here is incomparable to anything you have ever had before, varying from the best on Earth to the best across the galaxy. You will find multiple restaurants in the hotel, spaced apart as it is so big.
Just a few hundred meters away from the hotel is an activity center with a rooftop pool and an underground gym, for all the athletes. It is the perfect spacious getaway from the hotel with the gardens just outside of it with about 5 acres.
This pool is the best off-world relaxation spot to dive into, being 70 metres long and 20 wide it could hold a couple of hundred people. It includes two jacuzzis and a plunge pool, with a sauna, for those who like the quiet.
