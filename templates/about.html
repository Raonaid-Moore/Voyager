About Us

Our Hotels & Experiences

Welcome to the Voyager line of Hotels! We are the first and only hotel chain to reach beyond the stars and bring relaxation and fun to another level. We have a hotel branch on each planet of our local system, each with different activities revolving around each planets unique climate and terrain . We are a 5-star chain with state-of-the-art facilities and services unique to us. The views from our stations and rooms are out of this world! Every room has floor to ceiling windows allowing guests the best view of the stars and other planets.
