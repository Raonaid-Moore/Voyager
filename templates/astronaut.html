Requirements When Travelling to the Voyager Hotel

Your Space Outfit

Welcome to your adventure to the Voyager Hotel! As an esteemed guest, you will be provided with a state-of-the-art astronaut suit that ensures your safety and comfort during the trip. Here's what you need to know about your outfit:

  • Helmet: Equipped with a clear visor for optimal visibility and built-in communication systems.
  • Suit: Made from lightweight, durable materials to withstand extreme temperatures and radiation.
  • Gloves: Designed for dexterity and protection, allowing you to perform tasks with ease.
  • Boots: Anti-slip and insulated to provide stability and comfort during your journey.

Terms and Conditions

Before you embark on your journey, please review the following terms and conditions regarding the astronaut suit:

  • The astronaut suit must be worn at all times during the trip outside the hotel premises.
  • Any damage to the suit must be reported immediately to the crew for repair or replacement.
  • The suit is equipped with a limited oxygen supply; ensure you are familiar with emergency protocols.
  • Do not attempt to modify or tamper with the suit's components.
  • In case of an emergency, follow the crew's instructions and use the suit's built-in safety features.
